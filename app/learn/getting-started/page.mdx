## Understanding the Basics

Before diving into the growing process, let's cover some fundamental concepts:

1. **Mycelium**: The vegetative part of fungi that grows through substrate
2. **Substrate**: The material mushrooms grow on (grains, sawdust, etc.)
3. **Colonization**: The process of mycelium spreading through substrate
4. **Fruiting**: The stage where mushrooms develop and grow

<Callout type="warning">
    Success in mushroom cultivation relies heavily on maintaining sterile conditions and following proper procedures.
</Callout>
