## Materials & Tools
### Essential Items:
- **64-quart clear plastic bin**  
- **1-inch & 2-inch hole saw drill bits**  
- **Self-healing injection port grain bags**  
- **Sterilized grain spawn** (rye, millet, or wheat berries)  
- **Bulk substrate** (manure-based or hardwood blend)  
- **70% isopropyl alcohol**  
- **Nitrile gloves** and microfiber towels  

### Optional Upgrades:
- Fresh air exchange (FAE) fan  
- Ultrasonic humidifier with hose  
- Programmable cycle timer  

---

## Step 1: Construct the Monotub
### Drilling Air Holes:
1. **Mark positions**:  
   - **Lower holes (2-inch)**: 8.5 inches from base (2 per long side, 1 per short side).  
   - **Upper holes (1-inch)**: 4.5 inches from base (3 per long side).  
2. **Drill holes** in reverse mode to prevent plastic cracking.  
3. **Deburr edges** with a utility knife.

## Step 2: Prepare Grain Spawn
### Inoculation Process:
1. **Sterilize workspace**: Wipe surfaces with 70% isopropyl alcohol.  
2. **Inject spores**: Use 3-5cc of spore syringe or liquid culture into grain bags.  
3. **Colonize**: Store at 72-75°F for 14-28 days until fully white.  

<Callout type="warning" icon="⚠️">
    Discard bags with green mold, sour smells, or excessive moisture—these indicate contamination.
</Callout>

---

## Step 3: Mix Spawn & Substrate
### Substrate Recipes:
- **Manure-based**: 50% pasteurized horse manure, 50% coconut coir.  
- **Hardwood-based**: 80% oak pellets, 20% wheat bran.  

### Layering:
1. **Sanitize tub**: Wipe with alcohol.  
2. **Add substrate**: Layer 1-inch substrate, grain spawn, and repeat.  
3. **Top layer**: Finish with 1-inch substrate casing.  

---

## Step 4: Colonization Phase
- **Ideal conditions**:  
  - Temperature: 72-75°F  
  - Humidity: 90-95%  
  - Light: Indirect ambient light (12h/day)  
- **Timeline**: 2-3 weeks.

## Step 5: Fruiting & Harvesting
### Triggering Mushroom Growth:
1. **Introduce FAE**: Remove micropore tape from lower holes.  
2. **Mist daily**: Maintain 85-90% humidity with distilled water.  
3. **Harvest**:  
   - **Psilocybe**: Before the veil breaks.  
   - **Oyster Mushrooms**: When caps flatten.
