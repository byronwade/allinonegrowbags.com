## Top 5 Most Common Mushroom Growing Questions (Answered)

### 1. "How Long Does It Take to Grow Mushrooms?"
Most varieties take **3-6 weeks** from inoculation to harvest. Fast-growing mushrooms like oyster varieties can fruit in as little as 14 days after colonization.

### 2. "What Are the Easiest Mushrooms to Grow?"
- Oyster mushrooms (Pleurotus ostreatus)
- Shiitake mushrooms
- Lion's Mane
- White Button mushrooms

<GuideStep number={1} title="Choosing Your Mushroom Variety">
    Start with beginner-friendly species:
    
    - **Best for beginners**: Oyster mushrooms (fast-growing, resilient)
    - **Avoid**: Morels or truffles (require advanced techniques)
    
    <Callout type="tip" icon="💡">
        Google's "People Also Ask": *"What mushrooms can I grow indoors?"* Oyster, Shiitake, and Lion's Mane are top choices!
    </Callout>
</GuideStep>

<GuideStep number={2} title="Sterilizing Your Workspace">
    Contamination is the #1 reason for failure. Follow this checklist:
    
    1. Wipe surfaces with **70% isopropyl alcohol**
    2. Use a **still air box** (DIY with plastic tub)
    3. Wear **nitrile gloves**
    4. Flame-sterilize needles
    
    <ResponsiveImage 
        src="/sterile-workspace.jpg" 
        alt="How to Sterilize a Mushroom Growing Workspace" 
        caption="A contamination-free workspace setup for mushroom cultivation"
    />
</GuideStep>

<GuideStep number={3} title="Inoculation Process (With Exact Measurements)">
    For Google's "how-to" featured snippets:
    
    **Step-by-Step:**
    1. Shake spore syringe for 60 seconds
    2. Heat needle until red-hot
    3. Inject **3cc of solution** into grow bag
    4. Seal port with micropore tape
    
    <Callout type="warning" icon="⚠️">
        Pro Tip: Use 2-3cc per bag—this exact measurement answers the common search *"how much spore solution to use."*
    </Callout>
</GuideStep>

<GuideStep number={4} title="Ideal Growing Conditions">
    Target temperature-related searches (*"What temp do mushrooms grow best?"*):
    
    - **Colonization Phase**: 72-75°F (22-24°C)
    - **Fruiting Phase**: 65-70°F (18-21°C)
    - Humidity: 85-95%

<GuideStep number={5} title="Harvesting Your Mushrooms">
    Answering *"How do I know when mushrooms are ready to pick?"*:
    
    - Harvest when caps curl upward (before spores drop)
    - Twist gently at the base
    - Store in paper bags (not plastic)
